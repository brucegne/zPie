What is this all about?

I have been doing computer work since 1975. In fields such as insurance, banking, wholesale and shipping, and mental health care. But after suffering 3 heart attacks last year, I wanted to see if I still had the 'sharps' to design from scratch. This is a project I am using to test that out.
